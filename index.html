Dr Joseph Rosado and his distictive approach to Integrative Medicine

The Dr. Joseph Rosado Academy of Integrative Medicine provides education, primarily to medical professionals but also policy makers and the public, on the broad field of Integrative Medicine with a particular focus on the benefits of plant medicine and the science behind Medical Marijuana.

Provide The Best Education

The Dr. Joseph Rosado Academy of Integrative Medicine is named after its founder, Dr. Joseph Rosado M. D. - a leading physician based in Florida widely recognised from his many international public speaking engagements as a leading scientific figure in Medical Marijuana research, and for his many appearances on major US TV networks and leading podcasts promoting his book, discussing his work and speaking about his research.

A published author and respected educator, the Academy honors his contribution to the field and seeks to share his expertise so that the benefits of his knowledge can improve the lives of patients all around the world.
